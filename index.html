In a bubble and pie chart, there are four parameters. The two that determine the where the pie chart is are positional data. The other two are quantitative attributes that determine the size of the bubbles and the percentages within each bubble. The marks here are points and the channels are its size, position, and colors for each pie chart slice.

There are three parameters. The collection of different color points are a part of an item with a categorical name attribute (A-E). There is an ordinal attribute of each points ranking at which time from 1-5 and a quantitative year attribute.
